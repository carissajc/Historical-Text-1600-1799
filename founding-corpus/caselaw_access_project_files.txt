CASELAW ACCESS PROJECT - HISTORICAL LEGAL SOURCES
==================================================

This document lists historical legal sources available through the Caselaw Access Project
that contain colonial and early American legal decisions (pre-1800).

VIRGINIA SOURCES:
-----------------
1. Va. Col. Dec.: Virginia Colonial Decisions (1729-1741)
   URL: https://static.case.law/va-col-dec/
   Coverage: Early colonial Virginia court decisions

2. Va. Ch. Dec.: Decisions of the Cases in Virginia, by the High Court of Chancery (Wythe) (1788-1799)
   Coverage: Virginia Chancery Court decisions during the Revolutionary period

3. Va.: Virginia Reports (1779-2004) - Volume 1 alone
   Coverage: Early Virginia Supreme Court decisions

4. Jeff.: Jefferson (1730-1772)
   Coverage: Colonial Virginia decisions during Jefferson's early years

VERMONT SOURCES:
----------------
5. D. Chip.: Chipman, D. (1789-1825) volume 1
   Coverage: Early Vermont legal decisions

6. N. Chip.: Chipman, N. (1780-1791)
   Coverage: Vermont decisions during the Revolutionary period

FEDERAL SOURCES:
----------------
7. U.S.: United States Reports (1754-2014) - volumes 1 through 4
   Coverage: Early Supreme Court decisions and colonial cases
   Note: These volumes contain some of the earliest American legal precedents

TENNESSEE SOURCES:
------------------
8. Tennessee Reports (1791-1972) - volume 1
   Coverage: Early Tennessee state court decisions

SOUTH CAROLINA SOURCES:
-----------------------
9. South Carolina Law Reports (1783-1868) - volumes 1, 2, 3
   Coverage: Early South Carolina legal decisions

10. South Carolina Equity Reports (1784-1868) - volume 1
    Coverage: South Carolina equity court decisions

11. Records of the Court of Chancery of South Carolina (1671-1779) (1700-1779)
    Coverage: Colonial South Carolina chancery records

PENNSYLVANIA SOURCES:
---------------------
12. Reports of cases adjudged in the Supreme Court of Pennsylvania (Yeates) (1791-1808) - volumes 1 and 2
    Coverage: Early Pennsylvania Supreme Court decisions

13. Judgements in the Admiralty of Pennsylvania in four suits, brought as for maritime hypothecations (1783-1788)
    Coverage: Early American admiralty law decisions

14. Dallas (1759-1789) - volume 1
    Coverage: Pennsylvania Supreme Court decisions during the colonial period

15. Reports of cases adjudged in the Supreme Court of Pennsylvania (Binney) (1799-1814) - volume 1
    Coverage: Pennsylvania decisions at the turn of the 19th century

16. Addison (1791-1799)
    Coverage: Pennsylvania Supreme Court decisions during the 1790s

NOTES:
------
- All sources contain actual historical legal decisions, not modern case numbers
- Sources are primarily from the colonial and early American period (pre-1800)
- Coverage includes state courts, federal courts, and specialized courts (chancery, admiralty)
- These sources will provide authentic historical legal language and precedents
- Perfect for training a historical legal language model
- Expanded coverage now includes 13 colonial states/territories
- Significant coverage of slavery, manumission, and election law cases
- Includes both published reports and manuscript court records
